Tom Penny's Video Parts

Here is a selection of some of Tom Penny's most significant video parts over the years

Etnies Hi-5- 1995

This was a groundbreaking part released in the mid 90's which showcased his nonchalant flowing style and ability to perform switch tricks over handrails, which was extremely rare at the time.

es Menikmati- 2000

This 2000 release cam after Penny had created a buzz around himself in the USA before seemingly disappearing. It is in some respects a compilation of his footage from the 90's and cemented his reputation as a legendary figure.

Flip Sorry- 2002

With so much footage in Menikmati having been filmed years prior, Flip Sorry showcased Penny's skating after his move to France. Though Penny's skating itself had not really changed, Flip Sorry was a step forward for skateboarding in general, with Penny joining a roster stacked with a who's-who of world class street skateboarders.
